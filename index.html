				About me
					

					I have a Ph.D. from the Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology, at University Politehnica of Bucharest, in multimedia data
					processing, under the title "Automatic analysis of the visual impact of multimedia data". I am a researcher and member of the AI Multimedia Lab, 
					at the CAMPUS Research Center from University Politehnica of Bucharest, with research interests in multimedia processing, subjective properties of 
					multimedia data, deep learning, ensemble learning and deep fusion. I participated in 10 national, european or industry-focused research projects, and authored
					25 papers, including 4 Q1 journal articles.
